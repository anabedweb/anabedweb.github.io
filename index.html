About Me

Hi! I’m Anabel Bedlan, a second-year Computer Science student at Western Washington University and president of the Competitive Programming Club. I love building things, solving complex problems, and creating opportunities for students to grow through coding competitions and events. I’m currently a machine learning research assistant in WWU’s Hutchinson Lab, developing deep-learning models for astrophysical data. I’ve also worked as a programmer for the Pierce College Science Dome, contributing to interactive STEM tools and a NASA Space Grant project. Curiosity drives everything I do—whether it’s competitive programming, hackathons, or personal projects—and my long-term goal is to work in machine learning research.
